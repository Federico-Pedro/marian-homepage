Mariana Gabriela is an Argentinian selfmade artist who lives and work in the city of Tandil. Her artistic career began when she ramdomly grabed some pencils while being pregnant with her first child. She specializes in human figure and erotic art, her prefered means are color pencils and paper. At the moment she is working full time on a series of erotic drawings which will be shown during the course of 2025.
